partial pressure of H2O in air:

P = P0*exp(-MW*Lvap/R * (1/T - 1/T0))

P0=610 Pa at T0=273 K
or
P0=1 atm at T0=373.15K (boiling)

MW = 0.018 kg mol-1

evaporation rate:

dm/dt [kg/s] = (0.0888 + 0.0783*v)/Lvap * A * (Psat(T) - humidity*Psat(Te))

Lvap = heat of vaporisation [J kg-1] = 2.256e6 J kg-1 = 40.65e3 J/mol

v = wind speed [ms-1]
A = surface area [m2]
